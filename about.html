About Me

Nugwa’um Maxmuwidzumga!

My name is Carla Voyageur, and I am of the Musgamagw Dzawada’enuxw from Kingcome Inlet, BC. Maxmuwidzumga is the traditional name that I was bestowed, roughly translated to “I leave a part of myself wherever I go - my essence” from the Kwakwala language. I was raised immersed in the richness of the Kwakwaka’wakw language, culture, values and traditions. In contrast, keeping up with modern technology was instilled in me as a young child by my late grandfather who always ensured we had a computer. My extensive history of administrative and project management in the non profit, education and Aboriginal organization sectors gives me first hand knowledge to the needs and situations of such groups. This experience paired with my passion to design unique products and creative solutions gives rise to my being an extraordinary candidate to assist with bringing your vision to reality. As an Indigenous owned and operated technology business, I specialize in web design and development, graphic design in addition to consulting services in the range of marketing, group collaboration and developing digital growth strategies. To gain a sense of my work and style, please view my portfolio/s (graphic & website). I would love the opportunity to discuss your needs and how I can assist bringing your current initiatives to light.
